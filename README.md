## About Me

I'm an AI/ML Researcher at **Nuvo AI**, where I'm a core contributor to **NeuralIQ** — a clinical AI platform for stroke neurovascular care.

My work sits at the intersection of deep learning and high-stakes clinical decision-making: building, validating, and deploying AI pipelines that operate directly in hospital reading rooms, helping radiologists detect hemorrhages, identify vessel occlusions, and decide on treatment in minutes rather than hours.

I hold a **PhD in Physics from VNIT Nagpur**, which gives me an unusual edge: I approach ML problems with the rigour of a scientist and the pragmatism of an engineer.

## 🧠 What I Work On

### NeuralIQ — Clinical Stroke AI (Nuvo AI · Jan 2025–Present)

| Module | What I built | Result |
|---|---|---|
| **NCCT Hemorrhage Segmentation** | 3D U-Net pipeline trained on 3,000+ patient studies | DICE 0.73 (hemorrhagic), 0.58 (ischemic) |
| **LVO Detection on CTA** | Deep learning pipeline for Large Vessel Occlusion detection | **AUC 0.92 · Recall 0.87** — *Published: Medical Image Analysis, Elsevier 2025* |
| **Multi-modal Pipeline** | Unified NCCT + CTA stroke quantification | 44% reduction in door-to-treatment time |
| **Clinical Decision Support** | ASPECTS scoring, tPA/MT eligibility, midline shift | Deployed live in 47+ hospitals |

📋 **Filed IP:** 3D Deep Learning-Based Stroke Segmentation and Volumetric Analysis Pipeline for Clinical CT Imaging
